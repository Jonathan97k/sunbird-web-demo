About Sunbird Capital Hotel

Nestled in the heart of Lilongwe, our property represents the pinnacle of Malawian hospitality. Elegantly appointed rooms, world-class dining, and a dedicated staff come together to create an unforgettable stay for both business and leisure travellers.

Every detail has been thoughtfully curated — from the locally-inspired interiors to the Olympic-sized swimming pool and verdant tropical gardens. Whether you're here for a day, a week, or longer, our promise remains the same: exceptional service with warmth only Malawi can offer.

Enjoy fine dining at our signature restaurant, unwind at the spa, or conduct business in our state-of-the-art conference facilities. At Sunbird, every moment is an opportunity to experience something extraordinary.
